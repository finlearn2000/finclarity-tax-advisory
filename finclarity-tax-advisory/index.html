Our Expertise

Comprehensive Financial Solutions

Structured, reliable compliance and accounting services tailored for startups, small businesses, and independent professionals.

Bookkeeping & Accounting

Accurate, compliant, and daily bookkeeping on cloud-based Zoho Books or traditional Tally. We ensure your financial records are kept up-to-date and audit-ready year-round.

  • Ledger Management
  • Bank Reconciliations
  • Financial Statements

GST Compliance

End-to-end management of Goods and Services Tax compliance. Avoid fines and claim correct Input Tax Credit (ITC) with our regular reviews.

  • GST Registration
  • GSTR-1 & GSTR-3B filings
  • GSTR-2B reconciliations

TDS Returns

Complete handling of Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) calculations, deposits, and quarterly filings to prevent interest liabilities and notices.

  • TDS Calculation & Deposits
  • Form 24Q, 26Q, & 27Q
  • Form 16/16A generation

Income Tax Returns (ITR)

Yearly ITR planning, computation, and filings for individuals, salaried employees, freelancers, professionals, and corporate businesses.

  • Tax liability optimization
  • ITR 1 to ITR 6 filings
  • Advance tax computations

Company/LLP Incorporation

Launch your business on strong legal foundations. We manage all registrations, certificate acquisitions, and post-incorporation setups.

  • Private Limited incorporation
  • LLP registration
  • Partnership Deeds

MCA / ROC Compliance

Stay fully compliant with Ministry of Corporate Affairs regulations. We file mandatory annual updates, keeping your company active and in good standing.

  • Annual AOC-4 & MGT-7 filings
  • Director KYC & changes
  • Statutory resolutions & minutes

Virtual CFO & Strategic Advisory

Strategic management reporting, cash flow projections, and cost optimization programs designed to protect and increase your margins.

  • Cash flow forecasting
  • Management dashboard reviews
  • Cost control frameworks
